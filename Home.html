What is Illustration?

An illustration is a decoration, interpretation or visual explanation of a text, concept or process, designed for integration in published media, such as posters, flyers, magazines, books, teaching materials, animations, video games and films.This is typically created by an illustrator.

What is Character Design?

This one is the process which comes after the characterisation and consists in defining the character through his/her physical appearance.

We need to consider a character as a little fictional creature, human or not, that aims to please its creator and the public as well.

What is the background?

A background is part of a picture, scene, or design that forms a setting for the main figures or objects, or appears furthest from the viewer.

It is normally used to create an atmosphere, depth to the work or just to be appreciated, real or imaginary.
